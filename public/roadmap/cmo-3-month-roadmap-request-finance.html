The math

How doubling actually works

Before the to-do list — the math. Doubling paid users in 3 months requires attacking both new acquisition and existing conversion simultaneously.

Lever A — Convert existing free users

From sales data: Kiln, Houdini Swap, Novora, and dozens of others use Request for free (invoicing, AR). They've never had a demo or been shown the paid features. A targeted activation campaign can convert 10–15% of free users to paid within 30 days. This is the fastest path to revenue — no new traffic needed.

TARGET: 25–30% of your 2× goal from this lever alone

Lever B — New user acquisition

The remaining 70–75% comes from new signups converting to paid. Your current funnel: Traffic → Signup → Trial → Paid. The plan attacks all three stages: more traffic (SEO, referrals, paid), better signup conversion (new landing pages), and faster trial-to-paid (onboarding email + CSM handoff).

TARGET: 70–75% of your 2× goal from this lever
The solo-marketer constraint: You can't do everything. This plan is sequenced so that Month 1 fixes the conversion funnel (so every visitor is more likely to pay), Month 2 turns on acquisition channels (so more visitors arrive into a working funnel), and Month 3 scales what worked. Never run ads into a broken funnel.

Operating rules

Solo marketer principles

When you're one person, what you don't do matters more than what you do. These rules keep you focused.

Rule 1: Fix the funnel first

No ads, no outreach, no content until the core landing pages and conversion paths are fixed. Traffic into a broken funnel is wasted money. Month 1 = funnel. Month 2 = traffic. Month 3 = scale.

Rule 2: Ship ugly, iterate later

A GUSD landing page that goes live today with imperfect copy beats a perfect page shipped in 3 weeks. Use templates, AI-generated first drafts, and existing brand assets. Perfectionism is the enemy.

Rule 3: Measure weekly, not daily

Track 5 metrics every Monday: new signups, trial-to-paid conversions, organic traffic, demo bookings, and MRR. Everything else is noise. Put it in a single spreadsheet. Takes 15 minutes.

Rule 4: Leverage AI ruthlessly

Use Claude/ChatGPT for first drafts of every blog post, email, landing page, and social post. Your job is editing, strategy, and distribution — not writing from scratch. This 3× your output.

Rule 5: Say no to everything else

No conference sponsorships, no podcast tours, no brand redesign, no social media calendar with 5 posts/day. Those are Month 6+ activities. For 90 days, the only question is: does this directly lead to a paid user?

Rule 6: Sales team = distribution

Every asset you create must be instantly shareable by the sales team. A comparison page Thomas can drop into a chat. A video David can share in a TG group. Build for sales leverage, not marketing vanity.
